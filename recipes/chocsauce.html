Chocolate Sauce

Description

This is a delicious chocolate sauce recipe that is amazing on ice cream or any other place that chocolate sauce is called for.
You can't go wrong with this!

Directions

  • MELT: 1/4 pound of butter and 2 squares of baking chocolate in a double boiler
  • ADD: 1 cup of sugar 1 small can (2/3 cup) condensed milk
  • COOK: 15 minutes over moderately boiling water stirring occasionally
  • SERVE: Hot or cold over ice cream
